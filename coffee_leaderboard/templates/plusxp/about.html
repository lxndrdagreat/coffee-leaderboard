What is +XP?

+XP takes the base leaderboard system and adds a simple, experience-based "leveling" system on top of it.

Now, every recorded cup of coffee grants the user experience points, which earn the user levels, achievements, etc.

This is still a major work-in-progress!

How do I Earn Experience Points?

For each recorded cup of coffee, the following experience is calculated:

Starting experience: each cup starts with a worth of 100 experience points.

First cup of the day: the first cup drunk every day earns an extra 10 experience points.

Day Streak Bonus: for every day in row before the recorded cup that the user drank coffee, the user gains a bonus 5 experience points; the maximum bonus points is 35 (7 days). Note: this bonus only applies to the first cup recorded each day.

Late penalty: cups that are submitted late (via the -y and --yesterday parameters) are only worth 25 experience points, and do not receieve further bonuses. However, they do still count for the Day Streak Bonus for future cup entries.

This is the experience calculated for the first cup of the day, with a 3 day streak. 

    Starting score:    100

                      ----
             Total:    125

Leveling and Prestige Levels

As the user gains experience points, he will level up. There are 20 levels, but after the 20th level the user can level up once more to gain a Prestige Level. When a user gains a Prestige Level, his current level resets to 1, and the leveling continues.

A user's experience points a cumulative across levels and prestige, meaning it is possible to see not only what a user's current prestige and level are, but also the exact number of experience points they have gained over time.
